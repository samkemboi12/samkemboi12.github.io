Skills & Technologies

My technical skillset is centered around transforming raw data into meaningful insights, building reliable data workflows, and supporting data-driven decision-making. I focus not only on tools, but on how they are applied to solve real business problems. These are just a few of the tools and technologies I work with; I have experience with many more that I use depending on the project needs

SQL & Data Management

I use SQL to extract, transform, and manage data across relational databases. My work involves writing efficient queries, performing joins, aggregations, and ensuring data integrity for accurate analysis and reporting. Tools: MS SQL Server, MySQL, PostgreSQL.

Python for Data Analysis

I apply Python for data cleaning, transformation, and exploratory analysis. I work with libraries such as Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib to handle datasets, automate analysis workflows, and prepare data for deeper insights.

Data Analysis & Exploration

I analyze data to identify patterns, trends, and relationships that drive business outcomes. My approach includes data validation, exploratory analysis, and interpreting results in a meaningful business context using Python, SQL, Excel.

Business Intelligence & Dashboards

I design and develop interactive dashboards to monitor key performance indicators, track trends, and provide stakeholders with clear, actionable insights. Tools: Power BI, Tableau, Excel.

Data Visualization

I present data using clear and effective visualizations that simplify complex information. I use Matplotlib, Seaborn, Power BI, Tableau to ensure insights are easily understood by both technical and non-technical audiences.

Data Quality & Validation

I ensure data accuracy through validation, cleaning, and consistency checks. Tools: Python, SQL, Excel. This helps maintain reliable datasets that support trustworthy analysis and decision-making.

Advanced Analytics

I apply machine learning techniques to extend analysis beyond descriptive insights, building predictive models and uncovering patterns that support forward-looking decision-making. Tools: Python, scikit-learn, TensorFlow.

Business & Analytical Thinking

I connect data insights to business objectives by identifying key drivers, understanding performance metrics, and ensuring analysis aligns with organizational goals, combining data skills with strategic thinking.
