About Me

I am a data analyst with experience in transforming complex datasets into actionable insights that support both strategic and operational decision-making. My work is grounded in understanding the context behind the data, ensuring that analysis is not only technically sound but also relevant to real business challenges.

I have worked across finance and analytics environments, where I have been involved in data validation, migration, and dashboard development. Through this experience, I have developed a strong appreciation for data quality, consistency, and the importance of building reliable data foundations before deriving insights.

My approach to data analysis focuses on identifying key drivers, uncovering patterns, and translating findings into clear recommendations. I aim to move beyond surface-level reporting by explaining why trends occur and how organizations can respond effectively.

In addition to descriptive analysis, I apply advanced analytical techniques, including machine learning, to extend insights into predictive and forward-looking perspectives. This allows me to support more proactive and data-driven decision-making.

I am continuously developing my skills in data science, analytics, and business intelligence, with a strong interest in solving real-world problems using data. I am particularly interested in building solutions that combine analytical rigor with practical business impact.

What I Bring

Analytical Thinking

I approach problems with a structured mindset, breaking down complex questions into manageable components and ensuring that insights are grounded in data and logic.

Data Reliability Focus

I prioritize data quality, validation, and consistency to ensure that analysis is accurate and can be trusted for decision-making.

Insight Communication

I translate technical findings into clear, concise insights that can be understood and used by both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

Business Alignment

I connect data analysis to business goals by focusing on key performance drivers and ensuring insights support real organizational objectives.
